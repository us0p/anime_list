Functionalities:
1. List animes.
   - Filter by:
     - name
     - genre
   - The list should contain:
     - The cover.
     - A trailer link.
     - The sinopse.
2. Add animes to your list.
3. Animes on your list must be marked with one of the following tags:
   - To watch.
   - Watching.
   - Watched.
4. Anime discover and recommendation based on your list.
